About US

#Loveyourself

The first Self Defense session was held on 3rd of March 2018 at Iqra University EDC Campus, and an initiative was born. Empower Yourself was founded by Xenab Ansari as a personal campaign against bullying, violence and abuse. It has since then branched out into 6 of the most important Life Skills a person can master in Pakistan, to get through traditional and cultural barriers and succeed in the modern world. Along with her collaborators The Forge and Legally Yours, Xenab has delivered several workshops, lectures and projects to bolster the campaign and reach out to women and children in Karachi and Sindh.
