Projects

How many of us know exactly what to do during each minute of an assault?

How many of us can tell who a predator is around us?

How many of us know if the lawyer handling their case is on their side?

How many of us know how to avoid freezing up in sudden situations?

This and so many more questions arise when we think about basic survival skills. Here at Empower Yourself, we are not inventing new ways to stay safe, but bringing awareness to a host of incredible life skills that everyone can learn to be fearless and strong. We do not learn these skills at home, school or work. Some people are streetsmart and avoid problems, but for most people, we learn these important skills after many terrible experiences. We want to remove those experiences and trauma from your life by placing this information all around you, through Education and Media.

What happens when I learn everything here at Empower Yourself?

Our patrons have experienced a major paradigm shift during their workshops and have learn to be more confident, bold, brave and alert. Many women have reached out to us telling us they have left abusive homes, improved relationships, started businesses or even started studying after our workshops.
